Great Workout

CLimbing is an amazing full body workout, that everyone can do. The reason for that is because no matter how strong you are some things are impossable to to with just your arms. Climbing forces you to use your core to balance, will pushing with your legs and pulling with your arms. Your back and shoulders work overtime too.
